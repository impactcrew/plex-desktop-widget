# PlexWidget Icon Concepts V2 - "NOW PLAYING" Focus

## Design Brief
Icons that clearly communicate "this is a media player showing what's playing RIGHT NOW" - not just generic music symbols.

### Requirements
- NO Plex logo or chevron (>)
- Must clearly represent PLAYER or NOW PLAYING status
- Work at 16x16px menu bar size
- Orange/yellow gradient colors
- Modern, professional look
- Must communicate "current playback" not just "music"

---

## Recommended Approach

**Primary Recommendation: Concept 1 (Mini Album with Play Badge)**
- Most directly represents what the app does (shows album art)
- Play badge clearly indicates active playback
- Scalable and recognizable
- Professional and clean

**Secondary Recommendation: Concept 2 (Progress Bar with Dot)**
- Universal "now playing" metaphor
- Very clear semantic meaning
- Unique among menu bar apps

**Testing Recommendation:**
Create mockups of top 2-3 concepts at actual menu bar size with:
- Light mode menu bar
- Dark mode menu bar
- With adjacent system icons for context
- Test if meaning is immediately clear

---

## Key Differences from V1

**V1 Problems:**
- Waveform = just "audio" (static)
- Heart = too abstract, emotional
- Orbital = unclear meaning

**V2 Solutions:**
- Album + play = "playing this album NOW"
- Progress bar = "playback in progress"
- NOW badge = explicit current status
- EQ + play = "actively playing"
- Stacked cards = "current from queue"

All concepts now clearly communicate **ACTIVE PLAYBACK** and **CURRENT STATUS**, not just music/audio in general.
